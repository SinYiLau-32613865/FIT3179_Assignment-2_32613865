Intelligence Quotient (IQ)

IQ is a series of standardized tests designed to quantify human intelligence. This data visualization shows information about the world's average IQ.

Average Intelligence Quotient (IQ) by country

The map shows the average intelligence quotient (IQ) of each country. Higher average IQ scores are represented by darker blues, while brighter colours denote countries with lower average IQ scores.

IQ Classification




From this graph, half of the population has an IQ between 90-110. Small proportion of people IQ have IQ score that are extremely high (above 130) or extremely low (below 70). Membership requirement of Mensa is 98 Percentile or above.

Average Intelligence Quotient (IQ) by Continent






Based on this chart, it can be observed that Europe has the highestaverage IQ and Africa has the lowest average IQ.

Top Countries with the Highest and Lowest Average IQ

The bar chart on the right illustrates the top 10 countries with the highest average IQ. Most of these countries are continental Europe and Asia. Japan ranks highest, followed by Taiwan and Singapore.

The bar chart on the left shows the 10 countries with the lowest average IQ. Most of these countries are on the Africa continent. Nepal has the lowest average IQ.

Correlation between Population and Average IQ






Based on the plot, there is no obvious correlation between population and IQ. The population has two obvious outliers,China and India, with average IQs of 104.1 and 76.24, respectively.
